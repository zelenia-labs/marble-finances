## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please use GitHub's built-in [private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/yolandasantacruz/marble-finances/security/advisories/new) feature.

### Our Process
1. **Acknowledgment**: You will receive an acknowledgment of your report.
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the issue and keep you informed of our progress.
3. **Patch**: We will develop, test, and release a fix.

Please include the following information in your report:
- A detailed description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
- Step-by-step instructions to reproduce the issue.
- Any Proof of Concept (PoC) code or screenshots.

We appreciate your help in keeping our project secure.
